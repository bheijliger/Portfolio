Project 1: Machine Learning

This document outlines an individual coursework task related to predicting coronary heart disease (CHD) for males in a high-risk region of the Western Cape, South Africa. The dataset includes nine features such as systolic blood pressure, tobacco use, cholesterol levels, and family history of heart disease. The tasks involve exploratory data analysis, logistic regression with ridge penalty, and exploring other classifiers to achieve the highest accuracy..
